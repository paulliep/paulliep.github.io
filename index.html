Prizes

Two ways to win

If your photo is one of the top 20 highest-voted pictures, it will be submitted to an Panel, who will award a $250 Gift Card to the photo that best meets the contest criteria.

Plus, for every picture you upload, you are entered into the grand prize draw for a $1,000 Gift Card!

Just want to vote? Complete the one-time registration and you too will be entered into the grand prize draw!

How to Vote

Check out each submission and vote for up to 3 photos daily!

Every day, after reviewing the photo entries, vote honestly for the 3 photos you feel best meet the following criteria: Theme (50%) and Most Creative! (50%).

Each week, the 20 highest-voted photos will be submitted to an Panel, who will award a $250 Gift Card to the photo that best meets the contest criteria.

You can only vote for the same photo once per day—come back daily to vote again!
